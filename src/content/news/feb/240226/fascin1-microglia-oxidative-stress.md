title: "Protein Fascin-1 Shields Key Immune Cells From Dying After Spinal Cord Injury"
pubDate: 2026-02-24
brief: "Using single-nucleus RNA sequencing, researchers discovered that the protein Fascin-1 protects microglia — critical immune cells in the nervous system — from dying due to oxidative stress following traumatic injury. Preserving these cells significantly reduces secondary damage to the spinal cord and helps retain more function."
