
**The use of botulinum toxin for spasticity in people with spinal cord injury: a scoping review**
Research Category: Pharmacology / Symptom Management
Brief: This review evaluates the effectiveness of using botulinum toxin to treat muscle stiffness (spasticity) in individuals with spinal cord injuries. It highlights that while the treatment is widely used, more research is needed to create standardized guidelines and to better involve patients in setting their own recovery goals.
