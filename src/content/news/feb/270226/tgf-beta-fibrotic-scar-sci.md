title: "TGF-β: The Healing Protein That Builds the Scar Blocking Spinal Cord Recovery"
pubDate: 2026-02-27
brief: "The protein TGF-β plays a double-edged role after spinal cord injury — it seals the wound and stabilises blood vessels, but also triggers the dense fibrotic scar that physically blocks nerves from regrowing. Researchers are now investigating how to selectively dial down its scar-forming action while preserving its essential healing role, opening a new therapeutic target for recovery."
