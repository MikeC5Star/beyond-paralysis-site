title: "Engineered Biological 'Cargo Ships' Deliver Precision Therapy to the Injured Spinal Cord"
pubDate: 2026-02-27
brief: "Extracellular vesicles — tiny biological particles that naturally carry healing signals between cells — are being engineered to carry specific drugs or genetic instructions to the injury site. This review shows that combining these enhanced vesicles with other therapies allows them to bypass the body's natural barriers and deliver high-precision treatment that stimulates nerve regrowth and reduces damaging inflammation."
